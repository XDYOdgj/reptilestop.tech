GLOVEWORKS HD Nitrile Gloves: Your Reliable Partner in Tough Jobs

Engineered for heavy-duty tasks, these nitrile gloves deliver unmatched protection and durability. With an 8 mil thickness, they are built to resist cuts, abrasions, and chemical exposure, making them a dependable choice for mechanics, plumbers, and anyone working with tools or small objects.

The raised diamond texture on the palms provides a powerful grip, even in wet or oily conditions, so you can handle your work confidently and safely. Whether you're cleaning your home, preparing meals, or working on your car, these gloves offer a blend of comfort and performance that keeps up with your needs.

Unlike standard gloves, GLOVEWORKS HD Nitrile Gloves provide 3x more puncture resistance, thanks to their reinforced construction.
