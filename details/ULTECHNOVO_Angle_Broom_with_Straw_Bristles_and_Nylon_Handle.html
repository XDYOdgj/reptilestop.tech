Introducing the ULTECHNOVO Angle Broom

This compact and lightweight angle broom is perfect for everyday cleaning needs. It combines practicality with elegance, featuring a nylon handle with an embroidered design that ensures durability and a refined look. The natural straw bristles are gentle on surfaces yet powerful enough to remove stubborn dust, making it an ideal tool for cleaning books, keyboards, and other delicate items.

Effortless Cleaning for Every Space

The unique angle design allows for easy access to hard-to-reach corners and crevices, ensuring no dust is left behind.
