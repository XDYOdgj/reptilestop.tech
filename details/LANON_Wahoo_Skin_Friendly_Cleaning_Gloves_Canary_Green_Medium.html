LANON Wahoo Cleaning Gloves

These gloves are designed for everyday cleaning with a perfect blend of comfort, durability, and safety. Whether you're washing dishes, cleaning your kitchen, or tackling household chores, they provide reliable protection without compromising on style or usability.

Why Choose LANON Wahoo Cleaning Gloves?

  • Soft and Comfortable: Crafted with a cotton flocked liner, they offer a gentle touch that keeps your hands protected during long cleaning sessions.
  • Latex-Free and Safe: Made from premium PVC material and free from DEHP, these gloves are ideal for people with sensitive skin or allergies.
  • Non-Slip Grip: The unique snail pattern on the palm enhances grip strength, helping you hold onto pots, pans, and other items securely.
  • Durable Design: With a thickness of 26.6 Mils, these gloves are built to last through multiple uses, ensuring they stay strong and reliable.
  • Fashionable and Functional: Available in a fresh canary green color, they add an extra touch of style to your cleaning routine while being easy to put on and take off.

Versatile for Every Cleaning Job

From dishwashing and kitchen cleaning to car-washing and even clothes washing, these gloves are perfect for a wide range of tasks. Their reusable nature means you can use them over and over, making them an eco-friendly and cost-effective choice for daily use.

Quality You Can Trust

Backed by Intertek certification and compliant with CA Prop. 65 regulations, these gloves are made with the highest quality materials and meet strict safety standards. You can feel confident in their performance and reliability every time you use them.
