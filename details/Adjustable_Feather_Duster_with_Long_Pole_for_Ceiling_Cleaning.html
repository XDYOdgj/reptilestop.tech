Adjustable Feather Duster with Extendable Pole

Keep your home spotless without climbing ladders. This versatile duster is designed to reach high places and clean tight corners with ease.

Effortless Cleaning, Anywhere You Need It

This feather duster features an extendable pole that can be adjusted from 16 to 84 inches, allowing you to tackle dusting tasks at your own comfort level. Its ultra-soft, bendable head conforms to any surface or corner, making it perfect for windows, blinds, and even ceiling fans.

Hygienic and Durable Design

The duster head is fully removable and washable, so you can simply rinse it with warm water and let it air dry. This ensures long-lasting use and keeps your home clean without the hassle of disposable cleaning tools. Plus, the scratch-resistant microfiber material is gentle on delicate surfaces and won't cause damage.

Perfect for Detailed Cleaning

Whether you're cleaning your chandeliers, decorative picture frames, or the intricate parts of your ceiling, this duster is built for precision. Its flexible head ensures that every corner and edge can be reached without effort, making it an essential tool for any cleaning routine.

One-Handed Adjustments for Convenience

Enjoy the convenience of one-handed adjustments that let you easily customize the duster's length to suit your cleaning needs. This extends your reach safely and efficiently, saving you time while maintaining the quality of your cleaning.
