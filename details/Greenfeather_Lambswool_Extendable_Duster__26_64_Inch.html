Introducing the Greenfeather Lambswool Extendable Duster

This premium cleaning duster is designed for those who want to keep their spaces spotless with ease and precision. Featuring a soft, anti-slip sponge handle that provides a secure grip, it ensures comfortable use during every cleaning session. The extendable stainless steel pole offers a range of 26 to 64 inches, allowing you to reach high areas like ceilings, fans, and moldings without straining your back.

The large, fluffy lambswool head is ideal for capturing dust from various surfaces, including hard-to-reach corners and edges. Its anti-static properties help prevent dust from becoming airborne, making it perfect for cleaning sensitive electronics, bookshelves, or delicate interiors in your home, office, or car. The duster is built to last with a durable and lightweight design, and the easy-to-clean feature lets you wash and reuse it multiple times with simple water or neutral detergent. This versatile tool brings both style and efficiency to your cleaning routine, ensuring sparkling clean results every time.
